# SeigManager Contract

## Overview

SeigManager manages seigniorage distribution and staking rewards in the Tokamak Network.

## Contract Information

- **Network**: Ethereum Mainnet
- **Role**: Seigniorage calculation and distribution

## Key Functions

### Stake
Stake WTON to a Layer 2 operator to earn seigniorage rewards.

### Unstake
Withdraw staked WTON from a Layer 2 operator.

### Claim Rewards
Claim accumulated seigniorage rewards.

## Seigniorage Mechanism

- New TON is minted as seigniorage rewards
- Rewards are distributed proportionally to stakers
- Staking in Layer 2 operators earns additional rewards
