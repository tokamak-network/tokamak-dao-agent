# TON Token Distribution

## Overview

This document describes the initial and current distribution of TON tokens.

## Total Supply

- **Initial Supply**: 50,000,000 TON
- **Current Circulating Supply**: Subject to inflation from seigniorage

## Distribution Breakdown

The initial token distribution was allocated across various stakeholders:

- Team and Advisors
- Ecosystem Development
- Community Incentives
- Private Sale
- Public Sale

## Inflation

- New TON tokens are minted through seigniorage
- Inflation rate is governed by DAO decisions
